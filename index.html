Vector sketch animation generation with differentialable motion trajectories

This web-based supplementary material presents comparisons of the results of SOTA methods in converting input videos into vector sketch animations, ablation studies(power Basis vs. Bernstein Basis, with/without consistency loss, with/without motion heatmap), more experiments with long videos, 3D animation to sketch animation, and text-to-sketch animation. All videos will play in a loop automatically.
